Aircraft vector notices
=======================

This watch face uses the Font Awesome Free 6.5.2 "plane-up" and
"jet-fighter-up" icons by Fonticons, Inc. (https://fontawesome.com).

The icons are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
International license:
https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Changes: the original SVG path data was recolored, placed inside taller
transparent viewports so each aircraft rotates around the center of the watch
face, and rasterized into the PNG resources required by Watch Face Format's
packaging tools. The editable SVG masters are retained in the artwork folder;
the silhouette geometry is unchanged.
