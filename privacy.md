# Privacy Policy for Avionics for Wear OS

**Effective date:** July 21, 2026

Northstar Labs respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to the Wear OS watch faces in the **Avionics for Wear OS** collection (collectively, the "Apps").

## Information We Collect

The Apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data or other user information.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The Apps do not use analytics services, display advertising, or include third-party tracking technologies.

## Local Functionality

The Apps function locally on your device. Watch face features and settings are processed on the device and are not transmitted to Northstar Labs or third parties.

## Permissions

If an App requests access to a device feature or permission, that access is used only as necessary to provide the App's core watch face functionality. Information accessed through such permissions is processed locally on the device and is not collected, stored, transmitted, or shared by Northstar Labs.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

Northstar Labs may update this Privacy Policy if the Apps or applicable requirements change. Any updates will be posted on this page, and the effective date at the top of the policy will be revised.
